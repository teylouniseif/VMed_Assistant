Virtual Medical Assistant objectives

Physicians are pressured to spend less direct time with their patients, because of admistrative tasks and complex healthcare systems. This leads to issues of trust, misdiagnosis and unavailability of medical care. Virtual Medical Assistant aims to remove the bureaucratic burden from physicians and help them focus on medical care and the human interaction with their patients.

  • Extract valuable medical information from a conversation between doctor and patient.
  • Fill out medical forms with patient information.
  • Ensure there is no loss of information during doctor-patient interaction.
